How FoodBridge Works

01
📝

Donor Lists Food

Restaurants, caterers, and individuals list surplus food with details like quantity, expiry time, and zone.

02
🔍

Receiver Browses

NGOs and food banks browse available food filtered by zone and category, seeing real-time freshness levels.

03
📨

Request & Match

Receivers request food items. The system updates status instantly and notifies both parties.

04
🚚

Deliver & Fulfill

Food gets distributed. The dashboard tracks fulfillment, and expired items are auto-removed.
